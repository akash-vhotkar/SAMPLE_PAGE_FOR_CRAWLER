About Us

Who we are and what we stand for.

Stitch & Stride was founded in 2018 with one simple belief: that well-dressed men don't need to spend a fortune. We started as a small workshop in Portland, Oregon, stitching together timeless pieces that blend comfort with sophistication.

Today, we serve customers across North America with a carefully curated range of menswear — from everyday casual to sharp formal wear. Every piece in our collection is chosen with intention. We work directly with manufacturers who share our commitment to fair labor practices and durable craftsmanship.

Our team of designers studies both classic tailoring traditions and modern street trends to give you clothes that are neither trendy nor outdated — just right. We believe fashion should work for you, not the other way around.

Our Values

Quality Over Quantity

We'd rather offer thirty great products than three hundred mediocre ones. Every garment passes a thorough quality check before it reaches you.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner with suppliers who pay fair wages and maintain safe working conditions. We visit our factories and stand behind the people who make our clothes.

Sustainable Practices

Our packaging is fully recyclable, and we are actively working toward a carbon-neutral shipping model by 2027.

Customer First

Free returns, real support (not just bots), and honest sizing — because shopping for clothes online should be stress-free.
